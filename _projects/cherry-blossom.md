## Overview
Predicted peak bloom dates for cherry blossom trees in five countries.

## Methods
- Sequential threshold model based on chill accumulation and growing degree days
- Forecasting using time-series and climate data

## Result
Accurately predicted bloom dates; model demonstrates generalizable performance across multiple cities.
